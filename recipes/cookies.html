Sugar Cookies Recipe

Description

The best cookies to hit the streets. You are going to love these soft and tasty cookies.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Stir flour and baking soda together in a small bowl
  3. Melt 2 sticks of butter on the stove
  4. Pour melted butter and sugar in seperate bowl while stiring with electric stir
  5. Beat in Eggs and vanilla extract
  6. Gradually blend them now all in same bowl
  7. Roll into small balls and place onto ungreased cookie sheet
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes
  9. Let cool briefly then enjoy
