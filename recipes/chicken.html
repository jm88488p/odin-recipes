Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

The best crispy fried chicken you will ever have. The chicken will be juicy, flavorful, and golden deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • Choice of Chicken
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Put chicken in bowl and season with salt, pepper, and paprika
  2. Pour 2 cups of flour into a ziplock bag
  3. Season the flour with salt, pepper, and garlic
  4. Drop the pieces of chicken in the flour and coat them fully
  5. Fill a pot with oil until 1/2 full and heat until very hot
  6. Fill with as many pieces of chicken the pot can hold
  7. Let cook for 20-30 minutes on medium heat
